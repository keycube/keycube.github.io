True to its 3D nature, the keycube can now be explored from every angle directly on the [homepage](https://keycube.org)! An **interactive three-dimensional viewer**, initially [prototyped](https://github.com/keycube/k3web3d) by **Nolann Moisis** as part of an academic project (autumn 2024), has been simplified and embedded into the website.
This implementation is powered by the JavaScript library [three.js](https://threejs.org).

This is more than just a visual upgrade, it will serve as the foundation for future ways to experience the keycube and its features. Whether we are a potential user, a tech enthusiast, or simply curious, the viewer will give us a deeper appreciation for its thoughtful design and functionality.
