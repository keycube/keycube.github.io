Research Visualization

Heatmap 3D

Interactive visual analysis of the keycube studies, with per-participant preference scores and finger reachability mapped directly onto the 3D device.

Open Preference View Open Reachability View

What is included

  • 22-participant preference dataset with aggregate view
  • Per-finger and combined reachability analysis across all faces
  • Interactive 3D keycube viewer tied to study data

Explore the two study modes

Both visualizations share the same 3D model, but expose different interpretations of the studies.

Mode 01

Preference

Inspect individual participants or the mean of the full study. The viewer can isolate one face or show the whole keycube at once.

  • Participant-by-participant selection
  • Aggregate mean across all 22 participants
  • Per-face filtering with on-key score labels
Launch Preference

Mode 02

Reachability

Measure what each finger can realistically access, then compare totals, per-hand reach, or the best-performing finger for every key.

  • Per-finger heatmaps
  • Left hand, right hand, total, and best-finger views
  • Dynamic range adapted to the selected dataset
